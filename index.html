Hi!

Tim Berners Lee is a software engineer who invented the internet in 1989 while at CERM, the European Particle Physics Labratory. He is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a professor in the Computer Science Department at the university of Oxford. In 2001 he became a fellow of the royal society and in 2004 he was knighted

In 1994 he founded the World Wide Web Consortium at the labratory of computer science at the Massachusetts Institue of Technology in Boston. It was made of companies that wanted to work together to create web standards and also improve the web. It was quickly agreed that the technology should be free of royalties and patents. Tim Berners Lee has served as the director of this W3C since it was founded. In 1995 Microsoft produced the first version of internet explorer and this commercial broswer created enormous growth in websites and web-users. The web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
